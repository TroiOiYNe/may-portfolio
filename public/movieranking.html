The Best Movies According to MaY

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather (1972)

A cinematic masterpiece, "The Godfather" unfolds the powerful tale of the Corleone crime family. Marlon Brando's iconic performance as Vito Corleone and the film's intricate storytelling set a standard for crime dramas. A timeless classic that delves into power, family, and morality.

The Intern (2015)

A heartwarming comedy-drama, "The Intern" pairs Robert De Niro as a senior intern with Anne Hathaway's young CEO. The film explores intergenerational relationships, work-life balance, and challenges stereotypes. De Niro's charm and the film's positive message make it an enjoyable watch.

The Pursuit of Happyness (2006)

Based on a true story, Will Smith stars in this inspiring drama about Chris Gardner's journey from homelessness to a successful career. Heartfelt performances and a compelling narrative highlight the resilience of the human spirit, making it an emotionally uplifting and motivational film.
